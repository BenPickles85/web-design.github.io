Latitude vs Humidity

There is no correlation between humidity and latitude. However, it is apparent that for latitudes under -40 and above 55 degrees, there are no cities with humidity under 40%. This could make sense as cold air has a lesser ability to retain moisture than warm air. To further investigate this I ran temp vs humidity and although there was more of a correlation, particularly in the northern hemisphere which had an r value = 0.2, any relationship is still a very weak relationship.
