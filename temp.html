Latitude vs Max Temperature

There is a moderately strong correlation between temperature and latitude in both hemishperes. Although it is apparent that temperatures at the poles are on avergae cooler than at the equator, other factors such as altitude and proximity to the ocean also have an impact and limit the latitude/temperature relationship from being stronger.
